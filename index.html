Goal is an international association football news
website founded in 2004 by Chicco Merighi and
Gianluigi Longinotti-Buitoni. It is currently owned by
Footballco. Goal is the world's largest online football
publication, and second largest online sports
publication, only behind ESPN.wikipedia

Headquarters: London, England

Launched: 2004

Awards: Asia's Sports Industry Award for Best Sports
Digital Platform of the Year

Owner: DZN Group

Nominations: Asia's Sports Industry Award for Best
Sports Digital Platform of the Year
